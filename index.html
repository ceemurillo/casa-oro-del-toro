Nuestra Historia

Born from the Land of Zacatecas

Casa Oro Del Toro carries the soul of Zacatecas — a land of silver mountains, haciendas, and recipes passed down through generations. Our birria was born in clay pots over open flame long before it became a trend.

"De Nuestra Tierra Nace El Sabor, Donde El Toro Define El Sabor."

We slow-braise our birria for hours in our signature consommé, press our tortillas by hand every single morning, and serve everything the way it was meant to be — bold, fiery, and full of heart. From legendary QuesaBirrias to Birria Pizza and Hot Cheeto Birria Balls, every plate is pure Zacatecas.
